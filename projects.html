Portland Historic Races, July 4th, 2012.

July 2012 saw the 25th annual festival of historic speed at Portland Internation Raceway in, you guessed it, Portland, Oregon. Fans of historic racing came from all across the northwest to watch and participate in the Inland North West's favorite summer passtime.

The weekend-long event featured a full spread of classics. Vintage Formula-1 cars, American Grand-Am Series classics, vintage prototype road racers, and sports cars from all eras made their way around the 1.5 mile circuit. V8's flat-6's, and a host of highly-tuned 4 cylinder engines echoed through the surrounding neighborhoods, making for an event that was truly a sight, and sound, to behold.
